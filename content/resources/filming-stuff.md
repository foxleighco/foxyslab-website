This one's a bit off-topic — it's not smart home related, but I get asked about my setup a lot so here it is. This is the gear I use to film and produce the channel. I started out filming on an iPhone and have gradually upgraded over time, so don't feel like you need all of this to get started.

My studio is pretty small, so I've had to be quite deliberate about what I use and how it's laid out. Everything here has earned its place.
