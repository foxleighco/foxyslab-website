This is my core smart home setup — the hardware that keeps everything ticking over. My whole smart home runs off a single mini PC tucked away in a server room running Home Assistant OS directly. It's simple, reliable, and handles everything I throw at it without breaking a sweat.

If you're just getting started with a smart home server, you don't need anything fancy. A decent mini PC and a Zigbee dongle is all it takes. Here's exactly what I'm using.
