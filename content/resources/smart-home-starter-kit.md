Here's a good starter kit to get your smart home up and running. This is an Alexa-based system as it's the easiest route for most people to get started — you get a decent hub, some smart bulbs, a plug, and a couple of sensors, and you're away.

Note: if you choose a different ecosystem, I can't guarantee all of these products will work with your hub. That said, the Aqara sensors use Matter over Thread, so they'll work with pretty much anything that supports it.
